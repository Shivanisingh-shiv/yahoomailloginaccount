Accessing Your Yahoo Mail Account

To login to your Yahoo Mail account, you'll need:

  • Your registered Yahoo email address or username
  • Your current Yahoo account password
  • Access to your recovery email or phone (if two-step verification is enabled)
  • A supported web browser or the Yahoo Mail app
Remember: Yahoo will never ask for your password via email. Always verify you're on the official Yahoo login page.

How to Login to Your Yahoo Account

1

Web Browser Login

Visit mail.yahoo.com in any modern browser. Enter your Yahoo email/username and password to access your account. Check "Stay signed in" on personal devices for faster access.

2

Mobile App Login

Download the official Yahoo Mail app from your device's app store. Open the app, tap "Sign In", and enter your account credentials to access your Yahoo Mail.

3

Email Client Setup

Configure your Yahoo Mail with email clients using IMAP (imap.mail.yahoo.com) or POP (pop.mail.yahoo.com) settings. Use an app-specific password if you have two-step verification enabled.

Account Login Assistance

Having trouble accessing your account?

  • Forgot Password: Use Yahoo's password recovery tool to reset your password
  • Account Locked: Wait 24 hours or verify your identity to unlock your account
  • Browser Issues: Clear cache/cookies or try a different browser
  • Two-Step Verification: Ensure you can access your recovery email or phone
  • App Problems: Update to the latest version of the Yahoo Mail app
Security Alert: Always verify you're on mail.yahoo.com before entering your credentials. Beware of phishing attempts.
